Balerion

Balerion, called the Black Terror, was a dragon of House Targaryen. He was ridden by Kyng Aegon I Targaryen during the War of Conquest

Syrax

Syrax was a female dragon. She was ridden solely by Rhaenyra Targaryen. Her name came from a Valyrian goddess

Arrax

Arrax was a dragon ridden by Prince Lucerys Velaryon during the Dance of Dragons

Caraxes

Caraxes, also called the Blod Wyrm and Blood Worm, was the dragon ridden by Prince Aemon Targaryen during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and later by Prince Daemon Targaryen

Seasmoke

Seasmoke was a light gray dragon. It was large enough for combat during the dance, but still a young dragon, and more agile in the air than its older brothers.

Vermax

Vermax it was the dragon ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon. It prospered and grew every year. The dragon became grumpy when near snow, ice and cold. Vermax's color is not described in the books

Vhagar

Vhagar was a female dragon ridden by Visenya during the Conquest, alongside Aegon the Conqueror's Balerion and her sister Rhaenys' Meraxes
